Our Project

In today’s world of AI, understanding videos is no longer limited to humans. With the combination of computer vision and natural language processing (NLP), we can now build models that watch videos and describe everything happening in them. This technology, known as video captioning, has applications in accessibility (for visually impaired users), content indexing, video summarization, and surveillance analysis. This article explains how to build a model that takes a video as input and generates meaningful text descriptions for what happens throughout the video.

From conceptualization to deployment, we believe in pushing the boundaries of what's possible with machine learning, automation, and human-centered design.
